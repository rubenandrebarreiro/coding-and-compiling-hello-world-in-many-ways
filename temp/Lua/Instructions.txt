#### Instructions ####

1) Compile LUA file, running the command:
   - luac -o HelloWorld.luac HelloWorld.lua 
                      or
   - luac -o HelloWorld HelloWorld.lua

   - 1.1) It will generate the following file:
          - HelloWorld.luac and HelloWorld

2) Run/Execute LUA file, running the following command:
   - lua HelloWorld.luac
              or
   - lua HelloWorld
   
   - 2.1) It will be printed the following output on the terminal:
          "Hello World!!!"
